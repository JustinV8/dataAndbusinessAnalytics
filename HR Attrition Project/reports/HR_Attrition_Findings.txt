IBM HR ATTRITION ANALYSIS - KEY FINDINGS
=================================================================

OVERALL ATTRITION METRICS:
- Total Employees Analyzed: 1,470
- Overall Attrition Rate: 16.3% (240 employees left)
- Industry Benchmark: ~10-15% (company is above benchmark)

=================================================================
TOP 5 ATTRITION DRIVERS (BY IMPACT):
=================================================================

1. OVERTIME WORK
   - Employees with OverTime: 30.5% attrition rate
   - Employees without OverTime: 10.4% attrition rate
   - Difference: 20.1 percentage points
   - Impact: OverTime workers are 3x more likely to leave
   - Recommendation Priority: HIGH

2. JOB SATISFACTION
   - Low Satisfaction (1): 23.4% attrition
   - High Satisfaction (4): 11.0% attrition
   - Difference: 12.4 percentage points
   - Impact: Low satisfaction doubles attrition risk
   - Recommendation Priority: HIGH

3. YEARS AT COMPANY (TENURE)
   - 0-2 years: 31.1% attrition
   - 10+ years: 6.7% attrition
   - Difference: 24.4 percentage points
   - Impact: New employees 4.6x more likely to leave
   - Recommendation Priority: CRITICAL

4. MONTHLY INCOME
   - Bottom 25% income: 25.8% attrition
   - Top 25% income: 8.3% attrition
   - Difference: 17.5 percentage points
   - Impact: Lower pay = 3x higher attrition
   - Recommendation Priority: HIGH

5. WORK-LIFE BALANCE
   - Poor Balance (1): 31.3% attrition
   - Best Balance (4): 13.0% attrition
   - Difference: 18.3 percentage points
   - Impact: Poor balance = 2.4x higher risk
   - Recommendation Priority: HIGH
