SWOT ANALYSIS CONCLUSIONS


STRENGTHS

1. Strong Product Portfolio
Diversified across 70+ categories
Top categories have high customer satisfaction (>4.2 rating)
Growing demand in health, beauty, home categories

2. Large Customer Base
99,000+ unique customers
Presence in all Brazilian states
Established marketplace brand

3. Flexible Payment Options
Multiple payment methods accepted
Installment plans increase accessibility
Low payment fraud rate

4. Growing Revenue
35% YoY growth demonstrated
Increasing order volume and frequency




WEAKNESSES

1. Poor Customer Retention
84% customers never return
No loyalty program
Limited personalization/recommendations

2. Long Delivery Times
12.5 days average (vs 2-3 days for Amazon)
Negatively impacts customer satisfaction
Regional disparities (North region 2x slower)

3. Geographic Concentration Risk
65% revenue from 3 states only
Vulnerable to regional economic downturns
Limited penetration in 60% of country

4. Low Average Order Value
R$ 155 vs industry average R$ 200+
Limited cross-selling/upselling
Price-sensitive customer base




OPPORTUNITIES

1. Market Expansion (BIGGEST OPPORTUNITY)
North/Northeast regions = 70 million people, only 15% penetration
Potential +30-40% revenue growth
Lower competition in these regions

2. Logistics Optimization
Reduce delivery time to <10 days = +20% satisfaction
Regional fulfillment centers
Same-day delivery in metro areas

3. Customer Loyalty Programs
Increase repeat rate from 16% to 30% = +50% revenue
Subscription models (beauty, pet supplies)
Personalized recommendations

4. Category Expansion
Electronics, furniture showing high growth
Premium product segments underserved
Private label opportunities

5. Mobile Commerce
Smartphone penetration growing 40% YoY in Brazil
Mobile app could capture 30% of orders
Push notifications for promotions




THREATS

1. Intense Competition
MercadoLibre dominates (50% market share)
Amazon entering Brazil aggressively
Local players (B2W, Via Varejo) competing on price

2. Economic Volatility
Brazil GDP fluctuations
Currency instability (Real depreciation)
Inflation impacts purchasing power

3. Logistics Infrastructure
Poor road infrastructure in Brazil
High shipping costs
Regional accessibility challenges

4. Customer Satisfaction Decline
Delivery delays causing negative reviews
Review scores trending slightly down
Social media complaints damaging brand

5. Payment Fraud Risk
E-commerce fraud common in Brazil
Chargeback issues
Need for better fraud detection
